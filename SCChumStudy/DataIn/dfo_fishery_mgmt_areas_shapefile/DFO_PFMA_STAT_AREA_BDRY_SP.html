Dataset Extent

Latitude: 48.0° to 60.0°
Longitude: -139.5° to -113.5°

Additional Information

Purpose

Fisheries are managed under the authority of the Fisheries Act and the regulations made under the Fisheries Act. The Departement of Fisheries and Oceans Canada divdes the waters up into Management and sub-management areas in order to establish these regulations to specific areas
